Ribeye Steak

Description

Who doesn't love a good steak? With this recipe you'll be able to avoid the hassle long wait times at crowded steakhouses and enjoy restaurant quality steak from the comfort of your own home. With this easy to follow recipe you'll have every one begging for the secrets to your pan-seared, herb and butter ribeye steak!

Ingredients

  • 1 (24-ounce) bone-in ribeye steak, 2-inch-think, at room temperature
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
