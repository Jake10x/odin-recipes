Peanut Butter Cheesecake

Description

The perfect treat for all the sweettooths out there. Whether it's the Oreo crumb based crust, the delicious peanut butter cheesecake filling, or the ganache, chopped peanut butter cups, and peanut topping there's something for every dessert lover out there!

Ingredients

For The Crust:

  • 1/2 c. cocktail peanuts
  • 30 chocolate wafer cookies
  • 2 tbsp. light brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp. unsalted butter, melted

For The Filling:

  • 4 8 oz. blocks cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/2 c. sour cream, at room temperature
  • 1 c. creamy peanut butter
  • 1 c. granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2/3 c. heavy whipping cream

For The Chocolate Topping

  • 1/2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/3 c. heavy whipping cream
  • Cocktail peanuts, chopped peanut butter cups and flaked salt, to garnish(optional)
